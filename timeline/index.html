Bible Timeline

Creation, Garden of Eden

Creation

Book of Genesis

Out of Eden

After Adam and Eve sinned, they were banished from the Garden of Eden. They were no longer allowed to eat from the tree of life.

Noah, Great Flood

Great Flood

God was displeased with the wickedness of mankind and decided to destroy all life on earth. Noah was instructed to build an ark and take his family and two of every kind of animal on board.

Tower of Babel, Nimrod

Nimrod

Nimrod was a mighty hunter and a king of Babylon. He built the city of Babel and the tower that reached the heavens.

Tower of Babel

People tried to build a tower to reach the heavens, but God confused their languages and scattered them across the earth.

~2000 BC
Abraham

Abraham

Abraham moved from current Iraq area, to the promised land.

God's Covenant with Abraham

God promised Abraham that he would be the father of many nations and that his descendants would inherit the land of Canaan.

Isaac

Abraham's Promised Son

Isaac is the promised son of Abraham and Sarah. He was born when they were very old.

Abraham Tested

God tested Abraham's faith by asking him to sacrifice Isaac. Abraham was willing to obey God's command, but God provided a ram as a substitute sacrifice.

Jacob / Israel, 12 tribes

Twins

Esau and Jacob were twins, but Jacob was born second and was favored by his mother, Rebekah.

Birthright

Esau sold his birthright to Jacob for a bowl of stew. Jacob also tricked his father, Isaac, into giving him Esau's blessing.

Israel

Jacob name is changed to Israel after wrestling with the LORD. Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob are known as the patriarchs of the Israelites.

Twelve Sons

Jacob had twelve sons, who became the ancestors of the twelve tribes of Israel.

~1500 BC
Moses, Red Sea Parting

Moses

Moses was born in Egypt during a time when the Israelites were enslaved. He was raised by Pharaoh's daughter but later fled to Midian after killing an Egyptian. God called Moses to lead the Israelites out of Egypt.

Red Sea

God parted the Red Sea so that the Israelites could escape from the pursuing Egyptian army. The waters returned to their place, drowning the Egyptians.

Ten Commandments

10 Commandments

Moses received the Ten Commandments from God on Mount Sinai. These laws were written on stone tablets and given to the Israelites as a covenant between them and God.
